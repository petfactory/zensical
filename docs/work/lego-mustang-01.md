This project is a mix of Houdini & Unreal. The vehicle and cameras were animated in Houdini as were the smoke and RBD sim. I used Unreal for the edit and rendering. The scene is lit by a lowrez HDRI, that was also used as a cheap environment. The ground is basically a plane with a Megascans texture and some decals. Rendered with the path tracer in unreal 5.4 with low settings (each frame took about 6 seconds). The final edit was done in DaVinci Resolve.

Lego model: Ford Mustang by Stephan3321 on mecabricks.com
